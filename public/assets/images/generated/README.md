# Generated Campaign Imagery

## Purpose
These images are AI-generated editorial campaign visuals used for website mood, service illustration, and brand direction. 

## Portfolio Policy
They are **NOT** real client portfolio images. Real client work remains separated in the portfolio section. 

AI images are strictly allowed for:
- Hero campaign visuals
- Service page visuals
- Bridal aesthetic placeholders
- Editorial beauty mood images
- Online consultation visuals
- Commercial shoot mood visuals
- Hair styling detail visuals
- Homepage campaign imagery

They must never be presented as Laya's actual portfolio, real bridal clients, or before/after results.
